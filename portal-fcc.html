Getting the most from re:Bloom FCC

1

Plan with the generator each week. Select Family Child Care, enter your theme and group composition, and your complete mixed-age plan is ready in seconds. Every activity shows how each age group participates.

2

Your routines are your curriculum. The FCC Scope and Sequence shows how caregiving routines, mealtimes, and outdoor time address developmental standards. Read the Routines section in the FCC Scope to see your day as curriculum.

3

Download your FCC Planning Guide. The Monthly Planning Guide gives you a full-year overview for mixed-age groups. Use it alongside your weekly AI-generated plans to stay organized and on track.

4

Select your state in the generator to tag every activity with your state's ELDS codes automatically. This turns your weekly plan into a QRIS documentation record with no additional work.
